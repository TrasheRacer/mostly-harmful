An experiment in intersectional anti-racist solidarity

Who am I?

I am a British software developer, currently working in Munich. I'm experienced mainly in developing web applications and databases. I'm struggling to learn German.

I hope to invert the machinery of surveilance capitalism in the interests of social progress.

What is this?

The business model of technology giants such as google and facebook depends on providing a service to users, while exploiting data gathered on the user.

The idea of this application is to guide users safely through encounters with law enforcment, while creating a public-access database on the operation of the police state.

Sounds interesting, can I get involved?

As an explicitly anti-racist project, this platform must be run by and for the benefit of People of Colour and other marginalised groups.
