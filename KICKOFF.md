## Project overview
An open-source anti-racist research project on the police state, with the potential for future monetization.
### Goals
#### Mobile phone app (PWA)
Provide a user at-zero-cost with essential services for interaction with law enforcement officials:
* Audio/Location upload streaming
* Legal guide:        traffic stop, border control, stop-and-search, ...
* 'Kontrolle' upload: when, where, by whom, for what reason, ...

#### Desktop/tablet website
Allow a user at-zero-cost to access data which they have uploaded.
* From user:         ethnicity/gender (optional), incidental audio/photo/video recording
* From user's phone: nickname, date/time, location, 
* From the police:   id, name/rank/station, statements, audio/photo/video recording (optional)

#### Public-access database
Allow access to researchers and activist groups, either at-zero-cost or affordably-priced.  
Data may also enable development of active countermeasures to overpolicing.
